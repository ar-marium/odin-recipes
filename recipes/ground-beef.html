BRAZILIAN-STYLE SHEPHERD'S PIE

Tasty, creamy meat-filled pie, perfect for family reunions and meetings!

Follow the following instructions to make one!

INGREDIENTS

  • 2.2 lb (1 kg) potatoes
  • 1.1 lb (500 g) ground beef
  • 7 oz (200 g) mozzarella cheese, shredded or sliced
  • Olive oil, for cooking
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt, to taste
  • White pepper, to taste
  • Chopped parsley and green onions (cheiro-verde), to taste
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Peel the potatoes, cut them in half, and cook in salted water until fork-tender.
  2. Drain and mash the potatoes. Add the milk and butter, mixing until smooth and creamy. Set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until fragrant. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Season with salt, white pepper, and the chopped herbs. Continue cooking until any liquid has evaporated.
  5. Spread half of the mashed potatoes in a baking dish.
  6. Add a layer of mozzarella cheese, then a layer of the seasoned ground beef.
  7. Repeat the layers, finishing with mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 40 minutes, or until hot and lightly golden on top.
  9. Enjoy! :)
